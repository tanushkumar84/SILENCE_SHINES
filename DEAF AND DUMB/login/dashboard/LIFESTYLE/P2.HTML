LIFESTYLE HEALTHY HABITS FOR DEAF AND DUMB

100% EVIDENCE BASED HEALTH TIPS

Visualize Your Routine

Use visual schedules or calendars to plan your day. This helps in organizing tasks and managing time effectively.

Stay Active

Engage in physical activities like walking, swimming, or yoga to maintain good health and reduce stress.

Healthy Eating

Choose nutritious foods like fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid sugary and processed foods for better overall health.

Practice Sign Language

Regularly practice sign language to improve communication skills and connect with the deaf community.

Protect Your Hearing

Avoid exposure to loud noises and use ear protection when necessary to prevent hearing loss.

Mental Well-being

Take time for relaxation and self-care activities like meditation or hobbies to promote mental health.
